About Me

Hi, I’m Richmond Cowther from Ghana — an aspiring software engineer with a strong foundation in science and a growing passion for technology. I hold a BSc in Integrated Science Education from the University of Education, Winneba, and I'm currently pursuing a certificate in Frontend Pro Development at ALX.

With a background in science, I bring analytical thinking and problem-solving to every coding project. My tech journey began with a curiosity about how things work, which led me into the world of software development — especially frontend technologies, where I blend creativity with code to build user-friendly, responsive interfaces.

I'm a quick learner, eager to take on challenges and continuously expand my skills. I'm currently building practical experience in web development, user-centered design, and collaborative problem-solving.

What drives me is the opportunity to use software to solve real-world problems and improve people’s everyday experiences.

Outside of coding, I enjoy exploring emerging technologies, following trends in software development, and committing to lifelong learning.

I'm actively looking for mentorship, internships, or junior developer roles where I can contribute, grow, and make a meaningful impact.

Let’s connect — I’m excited about the future of tech and ready to collaborate on projects that make a difference.
